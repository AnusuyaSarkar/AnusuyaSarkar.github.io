Educational Background

I completed my Higher Secondary education in 2024 from Rahara Bhabanath for Girls, where I was introduced to a broad spectrum of subjects that shaped my academic foundation. I'm eager to advance in this field and apply my knowledge to create impactful technology-driven solutions.
